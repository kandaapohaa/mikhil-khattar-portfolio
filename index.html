(02) How I Work

Three phases. Zero guesswork.

01

Find the Leak

Your Meta says one thing. Shopify says another. GA4 tells a third story. I run a full tracking and attribution audit to find exactly where your data breaks, where platforms steal credit, and where revenue disappears between systems.

What I look for: Broken purchase events, duplicate conversions, missing UTM parameters, misconfigured CAPI, checkout tracking gaps, new vs returning customer blindspots.
02

Rebuild the Foundation

I don't patch. I rebuild. Custom GTM containers, server-side tracking, Meta CAPI with real customer data, GA4 event architecture, cross-domain tracking, offline conversion pipelines. Every data point flows where it should, clean and accurate.

What I build: GTM (Web + Server), GA4 custom events, Meta Conversions API, first-party cookie systems, CRM-to-ad-platform bridges, automated data pipelines via Zapier, n8n, and Google Apps Scripts.
03

Watch the Numbers Change

With clean data flowing, everything shifts. Meta stops optimizing for repeat buyers. Your cost per acquisition drops. Your dashboards finally match your backend. Campaigns start targeting the people who actually spend money.

Real results: 50% better attribution from first-party cookies. 22% revenue increase in one quarter. 92.13% tracking accuracy on Shopify. Platforms aligned within 15% of each other.
